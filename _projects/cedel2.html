--- title: "CEDEL2 (Corpus Escrito del Español como L2)" link: "http://cedel2.learnercorpora.com/" year: 2016 category: web ---

CEDEL2 stands for Corpus Escrito del Español como L2 ‘L2 Spanish Written Corpus’ and is a large database containing the language produced by learners of Spanish as a second / foreign language. Users can query and filter the database and download corpus texts.
